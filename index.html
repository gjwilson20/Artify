About Us


We provide a fun out of school club for primary aged children, between the ages of 5-11, to explore their creative side through a range of different media including paint, sketching, printing and 3D modelling materials.


We offer:

- Weekly clubs for different age groups.

- Learn how to use different media to create age appropriate art work.

- Creative experiences with like minded peers.

- Develop new skills while having fun!
